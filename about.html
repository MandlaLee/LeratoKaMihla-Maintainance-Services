WHO WE ARE

Building Trust Through Professional Maintenance

Lerato Ka Mihla Maintenance Services is a South African maintenance company providing reliable, professional and cost-effective maintenance solutions for residential, commercial and industrial properties.

We understand that facilities and buildings require continuous attention to remain safe, efficient and visually appealing. Our team works diligently to provide maintenance services that protect our clients' investments while minimising downtime and disruptions.

Our focus is simple: Deliver exceptional workmanship, professional service and dependable maintenance support.

Our Mission

To provide dependable maintenance services that improve the safety, functionality and appearance of properties while delivering outstanding customer service.

Our Vision

To become one of South Africa's most trusted maintenance service providers through professionalism, quality workmanship and lasting client relationships.

Our Values

Integrity, Professionalism, Reliability, Accountability, Quality Service and Customer Satisfaction.
